h1. About NAACL

The North American Chapter of the Association for Computational
Linguistics (NAACL) provides a regional focus for members of the
Association for Computational Linguistics (ACL) in North America
as well as in Central and South America, organizes annual conferences,
promotes cooperation and information exchange among related scientific
and professional societies, encourages and facilitates ACL membership
by people and institutions in the Americas, and provides a source
of information on regional activities for the ACL Executive Committee.
