The 7th Brazilian Symposium in Information and Human Language Technology (STIL) begins in University of São Paulo, São Carlos, Brazil on Sep 8, 2009. The conference is from Sep 8-11, 2009.

STIL 2009 received financial sponsorship from NAACL.
